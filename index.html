About Acadia Park

Acadia Park Equipment Co. was founded by a brother and sister who were inspired by their love of the outdoors. They started the company to offer high-quality outdoor products at a price more people can afford. Their mission is to spread a love for nature, adventure, and the outdoors through reliable gear and community connection.
